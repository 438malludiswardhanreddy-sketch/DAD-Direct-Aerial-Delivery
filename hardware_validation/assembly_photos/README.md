# Hardware Assembly Photos & Mappings
This directory acts as a placeholder folder for physical photography documenting the assembly of the Tarot 680Pro hexacopter frame.

---

## 1. Required Photographs for Final Year Project Reports
To satisfy final year engineering review committees, the following photographs must be captured and placed in this directory:
1.  **Frame Mount Complete**: Upper and lower plates bolted, arm tubes locked in the folding mounts.
2.  **ESC Soldering Layout**: Detailed close-up showing solder joints on the integrated copper power distribution plate.
3.  **Pixhawk 6C Mounting**: Showing the flight controller secured in the geometric centre of the top plate on anti-vibration damping foam.
4.  **Companion Computer Housing**: Pi 5 mounted in a ventilated case with USB-to-serial connections routed to the Pixhawk.
5.  **Perception Sensor Mounts**: Showing the Raspberry Pi Camera Module 3 and Benewake TFmini-S LiDAR rangefinder aligned on the nose mounting plate.
